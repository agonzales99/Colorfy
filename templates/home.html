Welcome to ColorFy, an improved colorblind filter for those who suffer from various different types of colorblindness. Our mission is to help people who suffer from colorblindness filter their favorite pictures, images or even memes and allow them to see the colors they cannot visually interpret.

With our advanced color filter system, you can upload your image and have it filtered within minutes with your original picture and the new picture side by side so you can make the comparison and see the difference.
